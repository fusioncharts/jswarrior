Level 2

You spot a crab in the room. This one is much larger, and can do quite a bit of damage if you're not careful. But it's no match for you.

Use warrior.check() to check what's in the next step. Use warrior.check() == "enemy" to spot the crab, and warrior.attack() to kill it.
