The JavaScript Warrior

Welcome to the JSWarrior Game. Unlike traditional games, you can't control your character through a keyboard or joystick. The only way you can control your character is through JavaScript. To beat JSWarrior you can't just mash buttons, you need to think up a strategy and program the strategy as a JavaScript function.

But what's the point? Sure you will probably get a great intellectual thrill of having programmed some primitive AI within 30 minutes. But if you complete JSWarrior you get a real reward!

If you complete JSWarrior, and show it to us at the FusionCharts stall in JSFoo 2013, we'll give you a cookie!! This is because you're just that awesome.

Yes, it's a real cookie, the kind you can eat, not document.cookie).

Start playing!

Please note you will need a Google account to play. Your email will be private, used to verify when we give you the cookie. And we hate spam and unsolicited email more than you do.
