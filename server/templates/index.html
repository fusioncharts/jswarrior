Play JS Warrior

We're not going to weave some elaborate backstory. You're a good guy, and your objective is to get to the end of the levels. However, the only way you can control your character is through JavaScript. To beat JSWarrior you can't just mash buttons, you need to think up a strategy and program the strategy as a JavaScript function.

But what's the point? Sure you will probably get a great intellectual thrill of having programmed some primitive AI within 30 minutes. But there's also a cookie (a real cookie, the kind you can eat, not document.cookie) if you finish the game and happen to be in JSFoo. Simply show us the completed game, and we'll give you a cookie.

Go forth and play!
